=====
Body

The body is the structural backbone of the hexapod. It provides six symmetric coxa servo
mounting points, an enclosed electronics bay, and attachment points for the antenna mast and
camera module.

Structural Features
--------------------

- **Central spine rib** — a longitudinal rib running the full length of the body interior
  provides bending stiffness and divides the electronics bay from the battery compartment.
- **Gusset triangles** — internal gussets at each coxa mount distribute servo reaction torques
  into the body shell rather than concentrating stress at the mount bolts.
- **Snap-fit access lid** — four snap-fit clips retain the dorsal lid panel; a 1 mm lip
  provides positive location. The lid can be removed with a flat tool in under ten seconds.
- **Ventilation slots** — two rows of 2 mm slots on the lateral faces allow convective
  airflow over the MCU and power board, limiting temperature rise under sustained operation.
